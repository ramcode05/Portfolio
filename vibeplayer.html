Technical Architecture & System Specs

The Audio Core Concept

Vibe Player was engineered to bridge the gap between locally archived audio collections and web-based media streams, featuring a fully sandboxed offline-first architecture.

  • Offline Metadata Indexing: Scans target directories recursively, reading binary audio packets (ID3 tags) to dynamically compile track titles, artist details, and album art without external web calls.
  • Integrated Converter: Integrates custom scripting hooks mapping to yt-dlp and ytdl-core, downloading YouTube audio streams directly into the local directory.
  • Equalizer Engine: Features a custom frequency tuning equalizer controlling multi-band sound models for tailored auditory profiles.
